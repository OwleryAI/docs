---
title: "Upload Proof of Delivery"
description: "How to attach PODs and other shipping documents to your loads in Owlery."
sidebarTitle: "Upload PODs"
---

Once a load is delivered, upload the signed proof of delivery (POD) so your shipper has it on file. You can also upload other documents like lumper receipts at any time — you don't need to wait until delivery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the load">
    In the left sidebar, click **Shipments (Loads)** and find the delivered load. Click it to open the drawer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Documents tab">
    Click the **Documents** tab inside the load drawer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your file">
    Drag and drop your file into the upload area, or click to browse.

    Accepted file types: PDF, PNG, JPG, GIF, BMP, WebP, TIF. Maximum file size: 20 MB.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the document type">
    In the preview dialog, choose **Proof of Delivery** from the dropdown (or another type if you're uploading a different document), then click **Confirm**.

    {/*  📸 SCREENSHOT: The upload preview dialog showing a document preview on the left and the document type dropdown on the right, with "Proof of Delivery" selected. Show the Confirm button.  */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the upload">
    The document appears in the **Documents** table with its type, filename, and upload date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can upload documents at any point in the shipment lifecycle — even before delivery. If you have a lumper receipt or signed BOL from pickup, go ahead and attach it early.
</Tip>
