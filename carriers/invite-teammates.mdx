Need someone else on your team to handle loads for a shipper? You can invite them directly from Owlery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Invite Team">
    In the left sidebar under **Management**, click **Invite Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a shipper">
    Click the shipper row to expand it. You'll see a list of teammates who already have access.

    {/*  📸 SCREENSHOT: The Invite Team page showing at least one shipper row expanded, with current team members listed (showing name, email, and Active/Pending status).  */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Click **Invite To Team**, enter the person's email address, and click **Invite User**. They'll receive an email from [support@owlery.ai](mailto:support@owlery.ai) with a link to sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track invite status">
    Invitees show as `Pending` until they accept and sign in. Once they do, their status changes to `Active`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Invitees must sign in with the same email address they were invited with. There's no limit to how many teammates you can add per shipper.
</Note>
