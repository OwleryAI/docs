Owlery supports light mode, dark mode, and a system option that follows whatever your device is already set to. System is the default, so if your OS is set to dark, Owlery already is too.

## Change your appearance setting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the account menu">
    Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a mode">
    Under **Appearance**, choose one of three options:

    - **System** — matches your operating system or browser preference. This is the default.
    - **Light** — always light, regardless of your system setting.
    - **Dark** — always dark, regardless of your system setting.

    Your choice takes effect immediately.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This setting is saved to your browser, not your account. If you switch browsers or devices, you'll need to set it again.
</Note>
