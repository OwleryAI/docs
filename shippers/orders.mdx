**Where to find it:** `Orders`Your POs and SOs — the starting point for every shipment.


### **Getting orders in**

- **Integration sync** — orders pull in automatically from a connected ERP, WMS, or EDI; trigger a manual sync any time.
- **CSV upload** — fill the Order Template and upload from the table toolbar.
- **\+ New Order** — create one at a time (Purchase, Sales, or Transfer).


### **Working the Orders table**

- Switch between **Table, Item Summary,** and **Calendar** views.
- Filter by status chip; **Show All** reveals every status.
- Click any row to open the **Order Drawer** with full detail across tabs.


### **Moving fast with bulk actions**

- **Bulk Plan** turns selected orders into loads in one step.
- **Bulk Edit Dates** and **Bulk Send** handle many orders at once.
- From any open order, hit **Quote** to jump straight to pricing with the order pre-loaded.
