**Where to find it:** `Settings → Integrations`

Connect Owlery to your other systems so data moves on its own.

### The three types you can connect

- **ERP / Order systems** — NetSuite, QuickBooks, SAP, Cin7, Katana and more. Pulls orders in and writes shipment data back.
- **Brokers (3PL / TMS)** — CH Robinson, Echo, TQL, RXO and many others. Get rates, tender loads, and track.
- **Direct carriers** — ODFL, Estes, FedEx Freight, Saia and more. Rate, create BOLs, and schedule pickups directly.


### How to add one

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick your system">
    Click **\+ Add Integration** and pick your system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the credentials">
    These vary by vendor — API key, OAuth login, or username/password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test & Save">
    Click **Test & Save**. Owlery validates the connection before saving, then syncing begins on the next poll.
  </Step>
</Steps>


### Keeping connections healthy

- Check sync status right on the Integrations page — **green** means the last sync worked, **yellow/red** means there’s an issue.
- Admins can trigger a manual **Re-Sync** from the integration panel any time.
- You can run **multiple integrations of the same type** (e.g. two NetSuite instances for different subsidiaries).

<Tip>
  If you change a password or rotate an API key with a vendor, update it in Owlery right away to avoid sync interruptions.
</Tip>
