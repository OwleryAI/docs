**Where to find it:** `Dashboard → Dock Scheduling`

There are two ways to create an appointment — from the Dock Scheduling page or from an existing order.

## Option 1: From the Dock Scheduling page

Use this when you're booking against a facility you don't have an order for yet, or when the appointment isn't order-linked.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dock Scheduling">
    Go to `Dashboard → Dock Scheduling` and click the **Add Appointment** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a facility">
    Select the facility from the autocomplete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose stop type and mode">
    Pick **Pickup** or **Dropoff**, then **Live** or **Drop**. Available options depend on how the facility is configured — see [Configure facility settings](/shippers/dock-scheduling/facility-settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time">
    Select a date on the calendar. Available slots appear based on the facility's operating hours and appointment length:

    - **Green slots** — available.
    - **Disabled slots** — already occupied or in the past.

    Pick a slot, or choose **Customize Time** to enter a specific start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link orders (optional)">
    Enter order numbers to link the appointment to one or more orders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter freight details (if required)">
    If the facility requires freight details, enter **pallet count** and **reefer temperature** min/max.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    The appointment is created in **NotScheduled** or **Proposed** status, depending on who books it and how the facility is configured.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option 2: From an order

Use this when you already have the order in Owlery and want to book against it directly.

<Note>
  This flow requires **Create Appointments From Orders** to be enabled in `Management → Settings → Shipper Settings` under the **Quoting** section.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the order">
    Go to `Dashboard → Orders` and click the order you want to book against.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the appointment">
    Use the **Create Appointment** action on the order. Facility and order data pre-fill from the order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm date, time, and details">
    Adjust the timeslot and any freight details, then submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Appointment statuses at booking

New appointments land in one of two statuses:

- **NotScheduled** — the appointment exists but no time has been proposed yet.
- **Proposed** — a time has been proposed and is waiting on facility confirmation.

From here, the appointment moves through *Confirmed → Arrived → Loaded/Unloaded → Departed*. See [Manage appointments](/shippers/dock-scheduling/manage-appointments) for the full lifecycle.
