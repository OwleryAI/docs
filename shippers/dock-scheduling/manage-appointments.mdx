Every appointment moves through the same lifecycle:

`NotScheduled → Proposed → Confirmed → Arrived → Loaded/Unloaded → Departed`

Open any appointment from the **Appointments** tab in Dock Scheduling to work through it.

## Propose a time

Bookers (shippers and carriers) propose a time to the facility.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the appointment">
    From the **Appointments** tab, click the appointment to open the drawer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Scheduler">
    Click the **Scheduler** tab. You'll see a date and time picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a slot and propose">
    Select an available slot and click **Propose**. Add a reason if needed. The appointment moves to **Proposed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confirm a time

Facility users confirm proposed appointments.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the proposed appointment">
    Facility users see every proposed appointment in the queue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the time (optional)">
    Change the slot in the scheduler if the proposed time doesn't work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirm**. The appointment moves to **Confirmed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If **Unmanaged Facility Appointments** is turned on for the facility, bookers can self-confirm without facility approval — see [facility settings](/shippers/dock-scheduling/facility-settings#unmanaged-facility-appointments).
</Note>

## Update equipment status

Facility users move each appointment through the gate lifecycle from the status stepper below the scheduler.

- **Arrived** — driver is at the gate. Timestamp is auto-recorded.
- **Loaded** or **Unloaded** — freight has been handled.
- **Departed** — driver has left. Timestamp is auto-recorded.

If a **Driver Late Reason** is configured on the facility, you can select it when marking arrival. See [facility settings](/shippers/dock-scheduling/facility-settings#driver-late-reasons).

## Edit appointment details

From the appointment drawer you can update:

- **Carrier and driver info** — name, phone, email, truck number, trailer number.
- **Notes** — free-text notes visible to everyone on the appointment.
- **Freight details** — pallet count, reefer temperature min and max.

Changes save immediately.
