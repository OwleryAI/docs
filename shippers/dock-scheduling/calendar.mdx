The **Appointments** tab in Dock Scheduling includes a calendar view, a filter and search bar, and CSV export.

## Read the calendar

The calendar appears at the top of the Appointments tab (or as a split view with the table).

- Use the date navigation arrows to move through days, weeks, or months.
- Click any appointment to open its detail drawer.
- The calendar respects the filters set on the table below — filter by facility, dock, stop type, or status and the calendar narrows too.

### Color coding

Appointments are outlined by equipment status:

- **Gray border** — not yet arrived.
- **Orange border** — arrived.
- **Blue border** — loaded or unloaded.
- **Green border** — departed.

## Filter by status

Use the status tabs above the appointments table:

**Not Scheduled · Proposed · Confirmed · Arrived · Loaded/Unloaded · Departed · All**

Each tab shows the count of appointments in that state.

## Filter and search

Above the table you can filter by:

- Facility
- Dock
- Stop type (Pickup or Dropoff)
- Appointment type (By Appointment or FCFS)
- Live or Drop
- Shipper name
- Carrier name
- Date range

Use the universal search bar to search across **order number**, **load number**, or **reference number**.

## Export

Once your filters are set, use the **Download CSV** action to export the filtered results.
