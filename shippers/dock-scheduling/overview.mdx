**Where to find it:** `Dashboard → Dock Scheduling`

Dock Scheduling lets you book pickup and delivery appointments at your own facilities and at partner facilities, confirm proposed times, and track equipment through arrival, loading, and departure.

## What you can do

- **Configure facility settings** — set operating hours, appointment length, capacity, and booking rules per facility.
- **Book appointments** — create appointments directly, or from an existing order.
- **Propose and confirm times** — carriers and shippers propose slots; facility users confirm.
- **Track equipment** — move each appointment through *Arrived → Loaded/Unloaded → Departed*.
- **View the calendar** — see every appointment on a color-coded timeline.

## Who uses it

Dock Scheduling is available to shippers, brokers, carriers with facility profiles, and standalone facility organizations. Shippers typically use it to:

- Configure their own DCs and warehouses so carriers can self-serve appointments.
- Book appointments at 3PL or partner facilities directly from an order.
- Monitor gate activity in real time.

## Before you start

- Your **facility profile** must exist before you can add appointment settings. See [Facility profile](/facilities/facility-profile).
- To create appointments straight from an order, turn on **Create Appointments From Orders** in [Shipper Settings](/shippers/shipper-settings) under the *Quoting* section.
